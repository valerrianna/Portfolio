Hi! I'm Valerie Nguyen! I graduated from the University of Ottawa with a Bachelor in Health Science. I am in the process of completing the Bootcamp for Web Development at Carleton University I am looking for a position in the industry to fuel my creativity and to further develop my career. On my spare time I enjoy playing video games, playing the piano and drawing. My portfolio should include an example of front-end development of the Run Buddy website and my art portfolio. I enjoy designing custom art and custom wedding invitations.


My career experience is within the pharmacy. I have worked retail pharmacy interfacing with mostly customer services and preperation of medications. I then moved on to a more specialised pharmacy focusing on long term care facilities. Within this role I am the liaison between the pharmacist and the nursing staff caring for elderly patients who require special care. I recently accepted a new position with a sister company focusing on assisting patient to acquire coverage for high cost medication and to assist patient financially on crucial medications that are not usually accessible.


I hope with my knowledge in health care and newfound skills in web development can be combined and ultimately improve patient care.
